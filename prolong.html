Prolong your writing.

Post last update: 29 July 2023
When we need to write an essay, letter, report, etc. There are moments we run out of things to write yet it’s required that we write long documents. This blog post contains tips that might help to prolong your writing when utilized correctly. Tips are listed below.

Source: Pixabay

1. Ask yourself questions

To expand a topic of discussion there might be a need to ask yourself questions about this topic. When writing a skill in an application letter such as the ability to drive. Ask yourself questions. When, Where, Why, What & How. Write when you learnt how to drive, where you learnt how to drive, why you acquired this skill, what motivated you to drive what type of car did you learn to drive, how were you taught, how did you feel when you first learnt to drive. This can provide a paragraph with a few more sentences to make you document longer. Web English
2. Tell a story

A story is an excellent way to increase content of your writing. Write about your past experience, what you read, heard, watched, etc. Details such as weather, time, surrounding noise, speed, & so on, matter when describing a story. An extraordinary story can make reading what you wrote interesting. Add jokes when you can to prolong & spice your writing.

3. Share what you know about a topic

We are all experts in a particular topic. Understand what you are good at, when there is a need to write a long piece of writing flex your skill by sharing your knowledge in detail. This can make a long written account. It could be something as simple as how to preserve food in the kitchen. I’m not an expert here however it’s a relevant topic that can captivate listeners for a while.

4. Share experiences

Share your experiences no matter how sad or embarrassing. It’s interesting to hear embarrassing experiences. You don’t lose anything when you write about past humiliations, sad or happy moments with people.

5. Past, present, future

You can write about your past, present & future. This can include ideas, dreams, daily activities, & so on.

Don't allow yourself to struggle next time you run out of things to write. It might be a work report, school assignment, business proposal, you name it. One of these tips can help to add numerous sentences to your writing. You have to try to find out. Remember substance is what matters not volume. Don't write long pieces of writing for the sake of it. Do this only when it's a requirement. A short piece of writing written in good English with quality content is better than a long empty written rant.
